Avalon

Avalon is a giant private corporation that owns and manufactures many of the technologies that were involved in getting us off Earth and into space! Avalon has research, science, and engineering divisions and since the United Human Council clause of 2150, we are the local security force policing the solar system. There are Avalon Patrol officers in each charted sector of space, and they are trained to deal with a manner of situations, from diplomatic and informative to lawful and orderly.

Avalon looks after its employees. If you are interested in operating under a patrol, or working under one of our divisions, contact your nearest careers and contribution department.
