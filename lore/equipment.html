Equipment

We're sending you in with standard Avalon security employee equipment (a pistol and baton), however you may be able to procure additional equipment on location.

Stun Baton

The tried and tested life insurance policy, this should help you out, unless you're cornered into a wall. It has done its job the past 50 years, and is still reliable today. Other weapons are preferred.

Security Pistol

Nothing beats squeezing a trigger of a classic double-action diplomat to deliver your message.

Security "Blaster" PDW

This energy based automatic weapon is good for attacking from a distance, and is very reliable, but doesn't do too much damage.

Shotgun

Not much needs to be said. It explodes faces, looks cool, and packs one hell of a punch. Your best friend from out the door all the way to hell.

Auto-9

This gun does a great job at clearing rooms as well as your ammo supply. The power hungry beast is hard in your hands, but it fires so fast your enemies will become a new gore smoothie.

Plasma Gun

An experimental Avalon energy weapon that shoots giant globs of face-melting plasma. This thing gets hot though, and you'll need special protection to use it without becoming a boiling milkshake. Rechargeable.

Sentry Gun

Set and forget for as long as there's ammo. There are both portable and non-portable versions of this beast. The portable ones don't need a constant source to operate, but only hold 200 rounds, while the non-portable ones are much harder to destroy, and can fabricate their own ammo supply. Thank god for open source 3D printing!

Stimpack

It'll stop you from dying, at least if you only have a few cuts.

Medipack

It'll stop you from dying even further. Your backpack can store one and you can gradually use up the included supplies.

O2 "Oz" Canister

Your environment suit is compatible with the Oz system air canisters that can partially refl your air supply. You might want to pack a few if you have to go 'outside'.

PDA

A handy device for keeping things in check. Also includes a handy messaging system and note logger. Handy!

There may be other undocumented devices, make sure you double check each area to find them.
